MUPIANS

Another way of living.

Who we ?

Homo Mupianis: Outvaders. Mupians believe most differences are cultural; Only one law: No violence. A few rules: No alcohol nor drugs. No petrol. No money. No fuel burning engines. Nothing to buy or sell. Mupians: Evaders from society.

Where

Aleutian Islands, Unalaska and other wild lands. Small nomad moving hidden villages. Hard to locate, we want to hide from unexpected contacts and visitors.

What do?

Hunting. Fishing. Playing Music. Together. We dance, and sing to celebrate. We sometimes trade some goods for survival. Water is our most precious sacred resource.

How life?

Multi-generational 3 storey houses. Wild food only. No alcohol. Living in a Mupian comunity is the key, no one can survive alone. No chief, no hierarchy. Violence is not tolerated, offenders are excluded and returned to society.

Feeling bad and sad in this material, elitic society. We felt like unwelcomed strangers. We decided to get away. Hidden in Unalaska
